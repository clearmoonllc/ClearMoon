Our Guiding Philosophy

Writing a book is a journey into the unknown. It's easy to feel lost, isolated, and overwhelmed by the sheer scale of the mission. At Lunar Aura, we are your mission control.

We provide the structure, the expert guidance, and the unwavering support to help you conquer self-doubt and transform your creative block into a polished, finished work.

The Author's Armory

Your mission toolkit. A suite of services for authors and researchers at every stage.

Developmental Editing

Big-picture feedback on plot, character, pacing, and structure to make your story commercially viable.

Ideal For: Novelists and memoirists with a completed first draft.

Copy & Line Editing

Sentence-level polish focusing on clarity, flow, grammar, and syntax for a professional finish.

Ideal For: Authors with a structurally sound manuscript.

Ghostwriting

A collaborative partnership to bring your story or expertise to life in your unique voice.

Ideal For: Experts with a story but limited time or writing skill.

World-Building Consultation

In-depth guidance on creating immersive and logical fantasy or sci-fi worlds.

Ideal For: Speculative fiction writers seeking unforgettable settings.

Press Release Writing

Crafting and distributing professional press releases to announce your book launch to relevant media outlets.

Ideal For: Authors seeking media buzz for their new release.

Marketing Copywriting

Writing compelling book descriptions, author bios, and ad copy that converts readers into buyers.

Ideal For: Authors needing to build their brand and market their work.

Technical & Journal Editing

Specialized editing for academic papers, ensuring clarity, accuracy, and adherence to target journal guidelines.

Ideal For: Researchers and academics preparing manuscripts for submission.
